How to Apply

Admission Process

Complete these steps to apply for the upcoming academic session.

Submit Inquiry

Contact our admission desk or complete the online inquiry form.

Provide Documents

Upload previous transcripts, ID documents, and student photograph.

Assessment & Interview

Applicants attend grade-level assessments and parent consultation.

Final Confirmation

Selected students receive offer letters and enrollment instructions.

Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed admission form
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Previous school academic records
  • Parent or guardian identification
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
